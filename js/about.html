About My Work

Hello!

I'm Cameron Silknitter I work in many areas of design dealing with typography, animation, graphics, and so on. I welcome you to explore my webpage as I show you my usual creative processes and history involving working with visual design language and the arts.

Personal History

I'm Cameron Silknitter I have been interested in graphic design for many years. I grew up in Red Lion, Pennsylvania and currently have been attending York College of Pennsylvania to further improve on my skills as a visual artist; I have also done photography in my past years as well.

Creative Process

I'm Cameron Silknitter While my creative process behind visual and graphic design work has certainly changed over the years; as of now I mainly work in Adobe Illustrator, Indesign, Photoshop, as well as iPad apps like Procreate for illustration and LumaFusion for animation.
